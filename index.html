The Problem

Teaching crystal structures shouldn't be this hard

Every chemistry teacher knows the struggle. Existing tools are either too complex for the classroom or too basic to be useful.

😩

VESTA & CrystalMaker are overkill

Built for PhD researchers, not high school classrooms. Students get lost in menus before they see a single atom.

💔

Static images don't cut it

Textbook diagrams can't show 3D spatial relationships. Students struggle with coordination numbers and void spaces.

💻

No web-based option exists

No installs allowed on school computers? No budget for software licenses? You're stuck with PowerPoint slides.

The Solution

Built for the classroom, not the lab

CrystalClass is designed around how teachers actually teach and students actually learn crystal structures.

3D

Interactive 3D Models

Rotate, zoom, and slice through FCC, BCC, HCP, NaCl, diamond, and more. All in your browser with WebGL.

✎

Guided Learning Paths

Step-by-step explorations: "How does close packing lead to FCC?" Students discover structures, not just view them.

🎯

Exam-Focused Features

Coordination number calculator, packing efficiency visualizer, tetrahedral & octahedral void highlighter — the stuff exams actually test.

Teacher Toolkit

One-click classroom demos. Embed in Google Slides. Export screenshots for worksheets and exams. Save hours of prep.

Zero Setup

No downloads, no plugins, no IT requests. Works on any device with a browser — Chromebooks, iPads, phones.
